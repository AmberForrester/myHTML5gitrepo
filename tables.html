HTML Table Colspan & Rowspan

Cellspan: The spans two columns. To make a cell span more than one column use the colspan attribute.

Example # 1 - th colspan is "2"

Name Age

Eve Jackson 57


Rowspan: To make a cell span more than one row, use the rowspan attribute.
